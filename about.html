About Me

Creative, detail-focused, and motivated by strong digital presentation.

I enjoy combining technical learning with modern design principles to create websites that feel thoughtful, polished, and easy to use.

Who I Am

I am a computing student with a growing interest in front-end development, interface design, visual hierarchy, and building websites that feel both modern and professional.

What I enjoy most is the process of turning a basic layout into something refined through better typography, spacing, colour control, responsiveness, and interaction.

I see web design and development as a space where logic and creativity meet, and that balance is what makes the work exciting for me.

Values

The principles that shape how I approach digital work.

Attention to Detail

The final quality of a website often depends on details such as spacing, contrast, alignment, hover behaviour, and clarity of hierarchy.

Continuous Improvement

I enjoy refining work over time, making each version more polished, responsive, and effective than the last.

User Experience

A website should not just look attractive. It should also feel intuitive, readable, and accessible for different users.

Areas of Interest

Future Direction

I want to continue improving both as a developer and as a creative thinker. My aim is to produce work that is technically strong, visually polished, and clearly intentional.

This portfolio represents that ambition by showing more than basic coursework requirements. It shows a standard I want to keep building on.
